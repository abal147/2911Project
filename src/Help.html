Welcome to the Sudoku Help Manual

Rules of Sudoku
To complete a sudoku puzzle, you must ensure that every row and column as the numbers 1 to 9 only occuring in them once,
and that each of the 3x3 squares have also have the numbers 1 to 9 only occuring once. Your goal is to fill the 9x9 grid with the numbers from 1 to 9

How to use this Sudoku game
When you begin this sudoku app, you will be taken to a screen with 4 buttons.
The first 3 are the difficulties at which you can attempt to solve auto-generated sudoku games, and the last option is a sudoku solver.

Difficulties
Easy - If you never played sudoku before come here and learn the game, you are given unlimited hints at this difficulty
Medium - For casual players that know the rules of sudoku and played the game before, you are given 5 hints
Hard - For veteran gamers who want a real challenge, to solve this puzzle, you are given 3 hints

Solver
The solver is a useful tool that allows you to input the numbers for your own sudoku, and it finds the solution to it.

Once in game
Once that you are in a game, after selecting a difficulty, you will be able to attempt to solve the sudoku puzzle.
You will be timed on how long it takes you to finish the puzzle. The timer can be seen in the bottom right corner.
On the right side of the screen, you are given a few options during the game.
These options are; Menu, New Game, Hint, Reset and Solve.

The menu button will take you back to the first screen, where you can select from the difficulties or the solver.
The new game button will create a new sudoku game of the same difficulty as the one that you are currently playing.
The hint button when pressed; or when you press the "h" key, will give you the correct solution to a random single cell,
if you select a cell before pressing the hint button, the selected cell will be the one that you get the answer to.

The reset button restarts the game. It gives you back the same sudoku game, but without the changes you have made.
Though, if you have gotten hints, they will stay on the board.

The solve button completes the sudoku puzzle. Selecting this will finish the game, and make you unable to press
the reset button.
